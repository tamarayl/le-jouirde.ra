Our Story

Le Jouir De.ra

In early 2020, Tamara founded Le Jouir De.ra, a restaurant and café located in a small corner of Bekasi. The name was carefully chosen: “Le Jouir” means “to enjoy” in French, reflecting the restaurant's goal to provide a pleasurable dining experience. “De.ra” stands for Delicious Rasa, symbolizing Tamara's concept of merging Western and Indonesian flavors.

A food enthusiast, Tamara introduced a unique concept combining Western cuisine with Indonesian dishes, bringing the distinct flavors of both cultures into one place. The first branch, with a capacity of 30-40 seats, offers an intimate, warm atmosphere with modern décor fused with traditional Indonesian touches..

Meet Our Store

Shortly after opening, the COVID-19 pandemic struck, forcing Tamara to adapt by offering takeaway and delivery services. She also introduced DIY Meal Kits, enabling customers to enjoy Le Jouir De.ra meals at home. Thanks to these efforts, Tamara managed to sustain her business and built a loyal customer base. After the pandemic subsided in 2021, Le Jouir De.ra reopened for dine-in services. Due to high customer demand, Tamara opened a larger second branch with 80 seats. This branch offered a broader dining experience, introducing the Chef's Table concept for an interactive culinary experience. In 2022, Tamara expanded further with the opening of a third branch in Jakarta’s business district, offering luxury dining and a premium menu.

In 2023, Le Jouir De.ra expanded to other major cities, including Surabaya, Bandung, and Bali. Each branch is designed to reflect the local character, such as Bali's open-air ambiance with views of rice fields. Despite growing into a luxury restaurant with branches in multiple cities, Tamara remains committed to her initial vision of creating happiness through food that unites Western and Indonesian flavors.

Today, Le Jouir De.ra is known for its unique and high-quality flavors, providing exceptional culinary experiences. Tamara continues to plan for further expansion, aiming to introduce Le Jouir De.ra internationally to showcase Indonesia’s rich flavors to the world. She envisions the restaurant as an ambassador for Indonesian cuisine, demonstrating how Western flavors can harmonize with Indonesian spices and cooking techniques. This goal is not just to expand the business but to share the richness of Indonesian culture through authentic, high-class food on a global scale.
